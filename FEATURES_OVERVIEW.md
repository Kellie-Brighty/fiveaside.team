# MonkeyPost Platform - MVP Overview

**A Complete Football Ecosystem Connecting Players, Clubs, Scouts, and Service Providers**

---

## What is MonkeyPost?

MonkeyPost is a comprehensive digital platform that brings together all aspects of football in one unified ecosystem. Think of it as a "LinkedIn for football" combined with an e-commerce marketplace, talent discovery platform, and grassroots football management system.

The platform serves as a central hub where:
- **Players** showcase their skills and get discovered by clubs and scouts
- **Clubs** manage their teams, recruit players, and sell official merchandise
- **Scouts** find and track talented players through advanced search and communication tools
- **Service Providers** (coaches, referees, trainers) offer their services and earn income
- **Grassroots Football** thrives through organized leagues, matches, and community engagement
